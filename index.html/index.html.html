Problems due to more screen time :

Note about Eyes :

If you spend 4 to 12 hours daily looking at screens for work, education, entertainment, or for timepass, it can negatively impact your eye health. Prolonged screen time is hazardous and can lead to adverse effects on your eyes, mental well-being, and overall physical health, which may become evident after several years.


1. Eye strain: Prolonged screen use can cause eye fatigue, discomfort, and strain due to focusing on a screen for long periods without breaks .



2. Dry eyes: Staring at screens reduces blinking, leading to dryness and irritation of the eyes .



3. Blurry vision: Focusing on a screen for too long without breaks can cause temporary changes in vision, leading to blurry or double vision .



4. Headaches: Eye strain from screen use can trigger headaches, particularly tension headaches or migraines .



5. Neck and shoulder pain: Poor posture while using screens can lead to discomfort and pain in the neck, shoulders, and upper back .



6. Blue light exposure: Screens emit blue light, which can disrupt sleep patterns and potentially contribute to long-term eye damage, though more research is needed on this topic .


Solutions to Minimize the problems :

1. Take breaks: Follow the 20-20-20 rule—every 20 minutes, look at something 20 feet away for at least 20 seconds.



2. Adjust screen settings: Reduce screen glare, adjust brightness and contrast, and increase font sizes to make viewing more comfortable.



3. Position your screen properly: Keep the screen at arm's length and slightly below eye level to reduce strain on your eyes, neck, and shoulders.



4. Use artificial tears: Use lubricating eye drops to relieve dryness and keep your eyes moist.



5. Consider blue light filters: Use screen filters or glasses with blue light-blocking lenses to reduce exposure to potentially harmful blue light.



6. Maintain proper body shape: Sit in a comfortable chair with good posture, and adjust the position of your monitor, keyboard, and mouse to reduce strain on your body.
